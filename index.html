Welcome to (mis)match.

Welcome to (mis)match, a visual brain game that will change how you think. If you love puzzles, thinking games, or just a good challenge, you’re in the right place.

Every image in (mis)match is distinguished by three properties such as color, shape and pattern. A “(mis)match” is a group of three images: for each property, the three images must either all match or all differ. You’ll have two minutes to find as many (mis)matches as you can.

But watch out! The properties you’re looking for will change on every level. You’ll see properties like size, rotation and even movement.

Enjoy the challenge!
